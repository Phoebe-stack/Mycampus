How it started

Early 2025. Prince was staring at his phone at 2AM, trying to find notes for an exam in six hours. They were somewhere in a WhatsApp group. Or maybe in that Google Doc someone shared weeks ago. Who knows.

He'd missed another deadline that week. Not because he forgot, but because it got buried in 500 unread messages.

It hit him: campus wasn't hard because the work was difficult. It was hard because everything was scattered everywhere.

So he built something. Just a simple way to keep notes in one place and track deadlines. Showed it to coursemates. They wanted it. Their friends wanted it. Students from other universities started asking for it.

That's how MyCampusPal happened. Not some grand plan. Just a frustrated student who built what he needed.

We're still students. Still adding features. Still figuring things out. But thousands across Nigeria are using it now.

What we're building

Campus shouldn't be this hard. The learning part? That's supposed to challenge you. But the stress of managing everything? That's what we're fixing.

We're on a mission to make academic success accessible to every student in Africa. Not just those with money for expensive tutors or premium tools. Everyone.

MyCampusPal levels the playing field. AI tutoring when you're stuck. Resources when you need them. Organization that actually works. Everything built for student budgets and student realities.

Your success should depend on your effort and dedication, not your wallet.

We're building tools that make your hustle count more.
